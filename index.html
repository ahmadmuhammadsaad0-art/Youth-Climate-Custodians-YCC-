About YCC

Youth Climate Custodians (YCC) is a youth-led, community-driven organization focused on education, grassroots action and local resilience. We guide communities to reduce waste, plant trees, conserve water and prepare for climate impacts.

Mission

To empower youth and communities through education, leadership and practical climate action.

Vision

A climate-resilient future where every community protects its environment, biodiversity and people.
