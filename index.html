About YCC

Youth Climate Custodians (YCC) is a youth-led, community-driven organization that educates, equips, and mobilizes local communities to adapt, mitigate, and build resilience to climate change. We support tree planting, waste management, climate education, and sustainable livelihoods.

Mission

To empower youth and communities through education, leadership and climate action projects that build resilience and protect natural resources.

Vision

A future where every community is climate-aware, resilient and actively protecting its environment for generations to come.
